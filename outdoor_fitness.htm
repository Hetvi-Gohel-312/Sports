Types of Outdoor Fitness Equipment

We offer a comprehensive range of outdoor fitness equipment to create complete fitness parks or complement existing recreational spaces.

Gym Strength Training Pull Up Bar

Ideal for upper body workouts, it targets arms, shoulders, chest, and core with bodyweight resistance.

  • Strengthens arms, shoulders, and back.
  • Improves upper body endurance.
  • Helps in posture correction.

Cross Training Push-Up Bar Grips

Enhances push-up performance by reducing wrist strain and increasing motion range for better muscle engagement.

  • Enhances grip for better push-ups.
  • Reduces wrist pressure and injury risk.
  • Increases chest and triceps activation.

Horse Rider

Simulates horseback riding to strengthen the lower back, thighs, and abdomen with smooth, low-impact motion.

  • Gives full-body low-impact workout.
  • Tones thighs, arms, and abs.
  • Improves flexibility and balance.

Exercise Bike

Cardio equipment that improves heart health, burns calories, and tones the legs with adjustable resistance levels.

  • Boosts heart health and stamina.
  • Burns calories and fat.
  • Tones leg muscles

ABDOMINAL BOARD

Slanted bench used for ab exercises, ideal for crunches and sit-ups to strengthen and define core muscles.

  • Targets and tones abdominal muscles.
  • Supports back during sit-ups.
  • Helps in achieving flat stomach.

Rowing Machine

Full-body workout machine that mimics rowing, building endurance while targeting legs, back, arms, and core.

  • Works 80% of body muscles.
  • Combines cardio and strength.
  • Improves endurance and posture.

Elliptical Cross Trainer

Low-impact cardio machine combining arm and leg movement, improving heart health and burning calories.

  • Low-impact cardio for joints.
  • Engages arms and legs together.
  • Great for weight loss.

Dual Action Leg Press

Targets quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes with both pushing and pulling leg exercises for full leg development.

  • Builds strong legs and glutes.
  • Enhances lower body flexibility.
  • Useful for muscle toning and rehab.

  • Encourages outdoor activity and connection with nature
