Options

Rubber Gym Flooring

The most popular material for weight rooms and areas where equipment such as treadmills, or weight machines will be utilized is rubber. It is relatively inexpensive very durable, and fairly easy to install. Rubber provides a durable and long lasting surface to underneath heavy weight equipment and exercise machines

Foam Gym Flooring

The most popular material for aerobic and cardio facilities, foam provides excellent fatigue relief for athletes. It is also the material of choice for most MMA and other martial arts and gymnastics facilities.

  • Solid and engineered options
  • Multiple wood species
  • Various finishes and stains

Luxury Vinyl Tile/Plank

Combines the beautiful look of hardwood or stone with enhanced durability and water resistance, perfect for high-traffic areas and moisture-prone spaces.

  • Waterproof options
  • Realistic wood and stone looks
  • Easy maintenance

Porcelain & Ceramic Tile
